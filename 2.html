ABOUT SPIDER-MAN

Spider-Man, whose real name is Peter Parker, is a beloved superhero from the Marvel Universe. As a teenager growing up in Queens, New York, Peter was an ordinary high school studentâ€”intelligent, introverted, and passionate about science. Using his scientific knowledge, Peter designed his own web-shooters, allowing him to swing between skyscrapers and immobilize enemies with synthetic webbing. He balances his heroic duties with school, relationships, and personal challenges, making him one of the most relatable and enduring characters in comic book history.
